- Existing position is still valid and profitable

**When to TRADE:**
- Clear technical confirmation (e.g., MACD crossover + RSI confirmation + price at key level)
- Strong trend direction
- Good risk/reward setup (at least 1:1.5)
- Market is open and liquid
- High conviction based on multiple indicator alignment
